Spirex-CV 625

Amoxicillin & Clavulanic Acid Tablets

Description

Spirex-CV 625 is a combination antibiotic used to treat a wide variety of bacterial infections. It works by stopping the growth of bacteria. This antibiotic treats only bacterial infections.

Composition

  • Amoxicillin 500mg
  • Clavulanic Acid 125mg

Usage

Take this medication by mouth with a meal or snack to reduce stomach upset. Use exactly as prescribed by your physician.

Precautions

Inform your doctor if you have any history of liver disease, kidney issues, or allergies. Not recommended for viral infections.
